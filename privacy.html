Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Tubalcain Advertising Agency ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our B2B lead generation services.

1. Information We Collect

From Solar Installation Companies (Our Clients):

  • Business name, contact person, email, phone number
  • Service area, pricing, and installation capacity
  • Payment information (processed securely through Paystack)
  • WhatsApp number for lead delivery

From Homeowners (Leads):

  • Name, phone number, email, location
  • Property ownership status
  • Solar system budget and timeline
  • Home safety assessment responses

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver qualified leads to our solar installer clients
  • To manage Facebook advertising campaigns
  • To qualify leads through our assessment system
  • To process payments via Paystack
  • To send performance reports to clients
  • To improve our lead generation services

3. Information Sharing

  • Homeowner lead data is shared ONLY with the paying solar installer client
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share leads with multiple installers
  • Each lead is exclusive to one client
  • Payment data is processed securely by Paystack (we do not store credit card details)
  • We may share aggregated, non-identifiable data for marketing purposes

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure data transmission via HTTPS
  • Payment processing through PCI-compliant Paystack
  • Access controls limiting employee access to sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Data Retention

  • Client business information: Retained for duration of service + 2 years for records
  • Lead data: Retained for 90 days after delivery, then securely deleted
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years for tax compliance

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Facebook Pixel for ad campaign tracking and optimization
  • Google Analytics for website performance monitoring
  • Essential cookies for website functionality

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paystack (payment processing)
  • Facebook/Meta (advertising platform)
  • WhatsApp (lead delivery)
  • Make.com (workflow automation)

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
