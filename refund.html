Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

At Tubalcain Advertising Agency, we stand behind the quality of our lead generation services. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued.

1. Service Guarantee

We guarantee delivery of the minimum number of qualified leads specified in each package:

  • Proof-of-Concept: 15-20 qualified leads in 7 days
  • Standard Weekly: 20-30 qualified leads per week
  • Fast-Track Monthly: 80-120 qualified leads per month

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • We fail to deliver the minimum promised lead volume in any service period
  • Delivered leads do not meet our qualification criteria
  • Technical issues on our end prevent campaign launch within 48 hours
  • You cancel Fast-Track Monthly package before the 15th of the month

You are NOT eligible for a refund if:

  • Leads were delivered as promised, but did not convert to sales
  • Your business failed to respond to leads within 24 hours
  • You violated our Terms of Service
  • Campaign was cancelled after leads were already delivered
  • Setup fees (these are always non-refundable once work has commenced)

3. Refund Amounts by Package

Proof-of-Concept (₦35,000):
  • Full refund if campaign does not launch within 48 hours due to our error
  • Pro-rata refund based on number of leads delivered if below minimum (15 leads)
  • No refund once 15+ qualified leads have been delivered
  • No refund for low conversion rates (this is not guaranteed)
Standard Weekly (₦70,000/week + ₦50,000 setup):
  • Setup fee: Non-refundable once onboarding begins
  • Weekly fee: Pro-rata refund if lead volume falls below 20 leads
  • Current week: No refund once leads are being delivered
  • Future weeks: Full refund if cancelled with 7 days notice
Fast-Track Monthly (₦250,000/month):
  • Full refund if cancelled within first 3 days and no leads delivered
  • Pro-rata refund if cancelled before the 15th of the month
  • No refund if cancelled after the 15th of the month
  • Pro-rata refund if total leads delivered fall below 80 in the month

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Email tubalcain.my@gmail.com with subject "Refund Request"
  2. Include: Client name, package type, payment date, reason for refund
  3. Provide supporting evidence (screenshots, lead quality issues, etc.)
  4. We will review within 3 business days
  5. Approved refunds processed within 7-14 business days
  6. Refunds issued to original payment method (Paystack account or bank)

5. Alternative to Refunds

Instead of a refund, we may offer:

  • Additional leads in the following period at no charge
  • Service credit towards future packages
  • Campaign optimization and relaunch
  • Extended service period to make up for shortfall

6. Lead Quality Disputes

If you believe delivered leads do not meet our qualification standards:

  • Report within 48 hours of lead delivery
  • Provide specific examples with lead data
  • We will review and either replace invalid leads or issue partial refund
  • Leads are considered valid if they meet all qualification criteria
  • Low conversion rate alone is not grounds for refund

7. Non-Refundable Items

  • Setup fees (₦50,000 for Standard Weekly)
  • Advertising costs already spent on Facebook
  • Consulting or strategy calls provided
  • Custom landing pages or forms created
  • Services already rendered

8. Refund Timeline

  • Request review: 1-3 business days
  • Approval notification: Within 5 business days
  • Refund processing: 7-14 business days from approval
  • Bank processing time: Additional 3-5 business days

9. Cancellation Without Refund

You may cancel service at any time, but refunds are only available under conditions specified above. All cancellations require 7 days written notice.

10. Contact for Refund Requests

  • Email: tubalcain.my@gmail.com (Subject: "Refund Request")
  • WhatsApp: +234 802 923 4994
  • Response time: Within 24-48 hours

We strive for 100% client satisfaction. If you're unhappy with our service, please contact us before requesting a refund. We're committed to resolving issues and ensuring you get value from our lead generation services.
