About Us

Who We Are

Tubalcain Advertising Agency is a B2B digital marketing agency specializing in lead generation for the Nigerian solar energy industry. We help solar installation companies acquire qualified homeowner leads through strategic Facebook advertising campaigns.

Founded to bridge the gap between Nigerian homeowners seeking reliable solar solutions and certified solar installers who can serve them, we operate exclusively in the business-to-business (B2B) sector. Our mission is to make solar energy accessible to more Nigerian homes by connecting serious buyers with professional installers.

Our Mission

To accelerate solar energy adoption in Nigeria by connecting qualified homeowners with certified solar installers through data-driven digital marketing.

Our Approach

We pre-qualify every lead through a comprehensive 13-question assessment covering property ownership, budget, timeline, and home safety requirements.

Our Guarantee

You only receive homeowners who are ready to install within 90 days, own their property, have verified budgets, and meet all safety requirements.
